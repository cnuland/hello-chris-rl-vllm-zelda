# Judges Rubric (weights & definitions)

- `progress` (w=0.4): movement toward quest milestones (Maku gate→bubble→dialog→key; dungeon entry).
- `dialog` (w=0.2): correct dialog navigation; minimal misclicks.
- `puzzle` (w=0.2): switch triggers, block alignment, cutscene start.
- `novelty` (w=0.1): new rooms/tiles covered.
- `efficiency` (w=0.1): progress per step; low backtracking.

Calibrate with 10–20 human-scored segments; log IRR.
